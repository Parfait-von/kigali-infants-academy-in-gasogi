About Kigali Infants Academy

A story of passion, dedication and excellence in early childhood education since 2005.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by a group of passionate educators, Kigali Infants Academy began as a small nursery with just 40 children. Today, we are one of Kigali's most trusted early childhood institutions, serving over 320 students across three campuses.

Our founding principle remains unchanged: every child deserves a joyful, enriching start to their educational journey. We combine international best practices with a deep respect for Rwandan culture and values.

Accredited by the Rwanda Education Board and aligned with the Cambridge Early Years curriculum, we consistently produce graduates who excel in primary school and beyond.

What We Stand For
